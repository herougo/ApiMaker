<p>This is the <b>discography</b> of <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canadian</a> <a href="/wiki/Rock_music" title="Rock music">rock</a> <a href="/wiki/Musical_ensemble" title="Musical ensemble">band</a> <b><a href="/wiki/Billy_Talent" title="Billy Talent">Billy Talent</a></b>. Included in this discography are three items that are from back when the band was known as <b>Pezz</b>. These items are <i><a href="/w/index.php?title=Demoluca&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Demoluca (page does not exist)">Demoluca</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Dudebox" title="Dudebox">Dudebox</a></i>, and <i><a href="/wiki/Watoosh!" title="Watoosh!">Watoosh!</a></i>. Besides those items, this discography documents every studio album, live album, EP, DVD and single released under the name Billy Talent.</p>
